Career Timeline

Four decades. Three continents. One playbook: growth with integrity.

LL.B, LL.M—both First Class First—University of Dhaka, then Bangladesh Civil Service 1982. I joined Grindlays Bank and spent 21 years across South Asia and the Gulf, ultimately heading Legal & Compliance, Gulf, and steering the ANZ Grindlays / Standard Chartered merger.

In 2003 I became CEO of IDLC Finance, transforming a single-product leasing niche into a multi-product finance house. In 2009 I took the helm at Mutual Trust Bank: assets tripled, branches quadrupled, digital core installed, NPLs held under 3 %. I chaired the Association of Bankers Bangladesh and sat on every major regulatory task-force.

Today I serve on fifteen boards—ACI, Summit Power, Trust Bank among them—lecture on Banking & Finance, and raise auction paddles for the Bangladesh Cancer Aid Trust. Certified at Cambridge, INSEAD, Berkeley, LSE; still a student who never stops turning pages.
